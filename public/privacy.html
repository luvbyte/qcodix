QCodix — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

1. Overview

QCodix respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and how your information is handled.

2. No Data Collection

QCodix does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All code editing and execution happens locally on your device.

3. No Account Required

QCodix does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information.

4. No Cookies or Tracking

QCodix does not use:

  • Cookies
  • Analytics tools
  • Tracking scripts
  • Fingerprinting techniques

5. Local-Only Processing

All HTML, CSS, and JavaScript code is executed directly in your browser. Your code never leaves your device and is not sent to any server.

6. Local Storage

QCodix may use your browser's local storage to save your code temporarily. This data remains on your device and can be cleared by you at any time.

7. Third-Party Hosting

If QCodix is hosted on platforms such as GitHub Pages, Netlify, or Vercel, those providers may collect standard server logs. QCodix itself does not collect or transmit your data.

8. Security

While no system is completely secure, QCodix minimizes risk by:

  • Running entirely client-side
  • Not storing or transmitting user data
  • Avoiding unnecessary permissions

9. Children's Privacy

QCodix does not target children and does not knowingly collect any personal data.

10. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated at any time. Changes will be reflected on this page.

11. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the QCodix website.
